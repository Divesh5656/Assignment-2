About Us

Background information about our website.

This project on World Weather Conditions is to explore the wider exchange and availability of official weather information on the Internet.This global website presents OFFICIAL weather observations and information for selected cities. We have came up with this idea because there are not a lot of websites which provide exciting information about the weather. Most of the other websites are very plain and simple we have tried and came up with a bright and vibrant theme for the website to attract our users for their daily use.
